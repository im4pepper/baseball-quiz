How It Works

This is a simple, multiple choice baseball quiz. The quiz goes like this:

  • 1. A baseball related question is posed, and possible solutions are provided.
  • 2. You will select from the possible solutions simply by clicking on one of them.
  • 3. Once you've made a selection, you will automatically move on to the next question.
  • 4. Once you've completed the quiz, your final results will be displayed.
